## Backlink Anchor Text Strategy

Vary anchor text to avoid Google penalties:

- **Branded (40%):** "VisaPath", "VisaPath.io", "the VisaPath guide"
- **Partial match (30%):** "visa requirements guide", "check if you need a visa", "free visa checker"
- **Exact match (15%):** "India to Canada visa", "do I need a visa for UAE"
- **Generic (10%):** "click here", "learn more", "this guide"
- **URL (5%):** "visapath.vercel.app/visa/indian-to-canada"
